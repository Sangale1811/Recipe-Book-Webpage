Recipe

  1. Cook the Hakka noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente. Drain the noodles and rinse them with cold water to prevent sticking. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over high heat. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Saut√© for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add thinly sliced onions and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they turn translucent.
  4. Add sliced bell peppers, cabbage, and carrots. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender yet crisp.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, black pepper powder, and salt.
  6. Pour the sauce mixture over the noodles and toss everything together using tongs or a spatula. Ensure that the sauce coats the noodles and vegetables evenly.
  7. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly, until the noodles are heated through.
  8. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
